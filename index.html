Research
Modelling drug release, degradation, and soft-matter systems.

Drug release from degrading polymers

PhD, University of Limerick · 2021–2025

Developing PDE-based and moving-boundary models for drug release from eroding and degrading porous structures. Combining continuum mechanics, asymptotic analysis, numerical simulation, and comparison with experimental data.

Nonlinear diffusion–reaction systems Moving boundaries Finite dissolution rate

Soft-matter & adsorption systems

ICMS & MAC-MIGS Modelling Camp · 2024

Built continuum adsorption–transport models for contaminant capture in packed-column systems. Reduced PDE systems via scaling and model reduction, implemented numerical solvers, and analysed breakthrough curves to guide design and operating regimes.

Packed columns Transport & adsorption Parameter estimation
